# SocialSparkV3

Documentation: Social Spark

Purpose
Social Spark is an application developed with the aim of providing users with social activities they can try depending on the time of day they enter. The goal of social spark is to get people to do social interactions it can be personal or work related when they input a time of day.

How the application was made
social spark was built using Kotlin Programming language and Jetpack Compose.

User interface on the Application:
•	User’s will first see an input field labeled “Enter Time of Day”, this is where they will put in the time of day.
  Then below the text field they will see two buttons.
•	First button is labeled “suggest” this is the button that processes the time of day they’ve entered to get a suggestion.

•	Second Button is labeled “Reset” the function of this button is to clear the text field so users can enter a different time of the day.

•	Lastly at the bottom they will get to see the suggestion they asked for.
How to use Social Spark:
Users will open the application, and they will put in a time of day in the text field provided.
After users have entered the time of day, they will press the “suggest “button which will give them back a suggestion on what they should do.
Once they have gotten their suggestion they will press the” reset” button if they wish to enter a different time of day.
